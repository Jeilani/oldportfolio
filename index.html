About Jey

Hello World. I'm a front end web developer. Since graduating from MSU Denver recently, I've switched gears and fallen in love with programming and its endless possibilites.

I've taken on the responsibility of being a student of programming, with a special interest in web development. I first was able to become an expert in JavaScript through learning it in a processing environment, which allowed me to materialize animation design ideas. Processing is what is responsible for the animation behind this div. If you didn't notice, the colored objects are following your mouse. Keep clicking anywhere around the div for more balls to appear. (Works better on desktop). I'm also experienced in jQuery and have utilized a variety of its functionality.

My current goal is to contribute to an organization that is forward thinking and implements current solutions to problems.

I am hungry to apply my skills in HTML, CSS and JavaScript and it's libraries to use.
